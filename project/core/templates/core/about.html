Integrative Manual Therapy

at Portland IMT

Kellye Nicol is a California transplant happily living with her family in Portland, Oregon since 2012. She has a BA from Cal State Northridge, IMTC from the Connecticut School of Integrative Manual Therapy (2009), and LMT certificate in Oregon under which she practices IMT. In addition to her continuing IMT education, Kellye actively takes Jones Strain-CounterStrain classes and Fascial Counterstrain courses.

A mother of 4, including a special needs child, Kellye feels privileged to be able to focus her practice on women and children and create a safe, supportive environment for health. Her personal interests include hiking, quilting, and sailing trips with her family.
